Privacy Policy

How we handle your data.

Effective Date: 1 March 2026 | Version 1.0

HealthOnTheGo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: username, email address, and password (hashed).
  • Health profile: blood group, genotype, allergies, and chronic conditions — only if you voluntarily provide them.
  • Location data: approximate device location, used solely to show nearby clinics. We do not store your location history.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, and session duration — collected anonymously to improve the app.
  • Appointment data: name, specialty, and chosen date/time for appointments you book through the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the HealthOnTheGo service.
  • To show clinics near your current location (location never stored).
  • To send appointment confirmations and reminders.
  • To improve app features based on anonymised usage patterns.
  • To comply with applicable health and data-protection laws.

3. AI-Powered Features

The Symptom Guide and Health AI Chat use the Anthropic Claude API. Symptom and chat inputs are transmitted to Anthropic's servers to generate responses. We do not store the content of your AI conversations beyond your current session. Please do not share identifying personal or medical details you wish to remain completely private.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only:

  • With service providers (e.g. maps, AI) under strict data-processing agreements.
  • When required by Nigerian law or a valid court order.
  • With your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, hashed passwords, and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure — use a strong, unique password.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your account data at any time via My Profile.
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection.

7. Children's Privacy

HealthOnTheGo is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has registered, contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you via the app when significant changes occur.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests: privacy@healthonthego.ng
